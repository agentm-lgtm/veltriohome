Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book a photography session? +
We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance to ensure availability and allow time for proper planning and consultation. However, we do accommodate rush bookings depending on our schedule. Contact us directly to discuss your timeline.
What is included in a typical photography session? +
Our sessions include pre-session consultation, professional photography during the agreed time, comprehensive post-processing and editing, and delivery of final images in digital format. Additional options like prints, albums, and extended sessions are available.
Do you offer outdoor and indoor photography? +
Yes, we offer both outdoor and indoor photography. We can work at your preferred location or recommend scenic locations based on your vision. We have experience with various environments and lighting conditions.
How long does it take to receive edited photos? +
Typically, edited photos are delivered within 2-3 weeks of your session. Rush delivery options are available for an additional fee. We prioritize quality over speed to ensure every image meets our high standards.
Can I request specific editing styles or adjustments? +
Absolutely! We offer various editing styles and are happy to accommodate your preferences. During the consultation, we discuss your vision and can show examples of different editing approaches to find the perfect match for your needs.
Do you provide makeup and styling services? +
While we don't provide in-house makeup and styling, we can recommend trusted professionals in the area. We also provide detailed styling guidance to help you prepare for your session and look your best.
What is your cancellation and rescheduling policy? +
We understand that plans change. Cancellations made 7 days or more in advance receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days are subject to a 50% fee. Rescheduling is available based on our calendar availability.
